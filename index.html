FDA Status Key
FDA Approved — Legal prescription drug, approved for human use
Rx/Compounded — Legal but requires prescription or compounding pharmacy
Research Only — Not approved for human use; sold for research purposes
Experimental — In clinical trials or very limited data available
Risk Level Key
LOW — Well-tolerated, minimal side effects in research
MODERATE — Some risk, requires informed use and monitoring
HIGH — Significant risk potential, experienced users only
VERY HIGH — Serious risk. Not recommended outside clinical settings
